🕵️ Spy Game Rules

Read once. Play smart. No arguments later 😄

🎯 OBJECTIVE
  • There is ONE SPY hidden among the players.
  • All other players are CITIZENS.
  • Citizens win by correctly identifying the Spy.
  • The Spy wins by blending in and avoiding suspicion.
  • The phone acts as the HOST. No human knows all secrets.
🔁 GAME FLOW
  • Players enter names and choose a game mode.
  • The phone is passed one-by-one for secret chits.
  • Each player silently reads their chit.
  • After all chits are seen, discussion begins.
  • Everyone gives ONE short description in order.
  • After discussion, decide who won the round.
🕵️ MODE 1: BLANK CHIT (CLASSIC)
  • All Citizens receive the SAME WORD.
  • The Spy receives a BLANK chit.
  • Important Rule: The first speaker is NEVER the Spy.
  • Citizens describe the word without saying it.
  • The Spy must guess the word from others’ hints.
  • Spy should stay vague and avoid details.
Example: Word = "Pizza"
Citizens: "Cheesy, usually round"
Spy: "Something people enjoy together"
🎭 MODE 2: RELATED WORDS
  • Two similar but different words are used.
  • Citizens get Word A (e.g. Pizza).
  • Spy gets Word B (e.g. Burger).
  • Everyone thinks they are safe — including the Spy.
  • Small detail mismatches expose the Spy.
  • Over-specific answers are dangerous.
Pizza vs Burger
"Has slices" ❌ suspicious
"Fast food people love" ✅ safer
❓ MODE 3: QUESTIONS
  • Two similar questions are used.
  • Citizens answer Question A.
  • Spy answers Question B.
  • Answers must be natural and honest.
  • No fixed word exists in this mode.
  • Listen for answers that feel “off-topic”.
Question A: Favorite Hobby?
Question B: Favorite Sport?
Spy may mention cricket while others mention painting.
🗣️ DESCRIPTION RULES
  • Each player speaks ONCE per round.
  • If players feel unsure, they may agree to play another description round before voting.
  • Maximum: 1-2 words or ONE short phrase.
  • Speak in the order shown on screen.
  • No interruptions or side comments.
🗳️ VOTING & REVEAL
  • After discussion rounds are complete, players move to voting.
  • Each player votes for ONE suspected Spy.
  • Votes are counted openly.
  • The player with the most votes is accused as the Spy.
  • If there is a tie, description giving rounds continue.
🚫 STRICTLY NOT ALLOWED
  • ❌ Saying the exact word or spelling it.
  • ❌ Using actions, sounds, or gestures.
  • ❌ Copying another player's description.
  • ❌ Changing your answer after hearing others.
🏆 SCORING
  • If Citizens win → all Citizens get +1 point.
  • If Spy wins → the Spy gets +2 points.
  • Scores carry forward across rounds.
  • Final winner has the highest total score.
🏁 WIN CONDITIONS
  • If the accused player is NOT the Spy → Spy wins immediately.
  • If the accused player IS the Spy → Citizens win immediately.
💡 Tip: Stay calm. Overthinking exposes the Spy faster than silence.
